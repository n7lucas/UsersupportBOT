# Customer Support Policy Guide

**Company:** AssisMarket Online Retail
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

# 1. Overview

This document outlines the official customer support policies for AssisMarket Online Retail. It is intended to guide support agents and automated systems when assisting customers with inquiries related to orders, shipping, returns, refunds, cancellations, and account issues.

All support interactions must comply with the policies described below.

---

# 2. Order Processing Policy

### 2.1 Order Confirmation

After a customer places an order, they will receive an order confirmation email containing:

* Order number
* Itemized list of purchased products
* Shipping address
* Estimated delivery date
* Payment confirmation

Orders typically enter processing within **1–2 hours** after payment confirmation.

### 2.2 Order Processing Time

Standard order processing times:

| Order Type        | Processing Time           |
| ----------------- | ------------------------- |
| Standard Orders   | 1–2 business days         |
| High-demand items | 2–4 business days         |
| Pre-orders        | Processed on release date |

Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

---

# 3. Shipping Policy

### 3.1 Shipping Methods

AssisMarket offers the following shipping options:

| Shipping Method   | Delivery Time     | Cost   |
| ----------------- | ----------------- | ------ |
| Standard Shipping | 5–7 business days | $4.99  |
| Express Shipping  | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Next-Day Shipping | 1 business day    | $24.99 |

Orders over **$75 qualify for free standard shipping**.

### 3.2 Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship to:

* United States
* Canada
* United Kingdom
* European Union countries

We do **not ship to P.O. boxes for express or next-day shipping**.

### 3.3 Shipping Delays

Shipping delays may occur due to:

* Weather conditions
* Carrier issues
* Customs clearance for international shipments
* High seasonal demand

Customers should contact support if an order is **more than 5 business days past the estimated delivery date**.

---

# 4. Order Cancellation Policy

### 4.1 Cancellation Window

Customers may cancel an order **within 2 hours of placing it** if the order has not entered fulfillment.

### 4.2 Orders Already Shipped

If the order has already been shipped:

* It cannot be cancelled.
* The customer must follow the **return process** after delivery.

### 4.3 How to Cancel

Customers can cancel orders by:

* Visiting the **Order History** page in their account
* Clicking **Cancel Order**
* Contacting customer support

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within **3–5 business days**.

---

# 5. Return Policy

### 5.1 Return Window

Customers may return most items within **30 days of delivery**.

Items must be:

* Unused
* In original packaging
* With all accessories and documentation included

### 5.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

* Gift cards
* Downloadable software products
* Personal care products that have been opened
* Final sale items

### 5.3 Return Process

Steps to return an item:

1. Go to **Orders** in the customer account.
2. Select the item to return.
3. Choose a return reason.
4. Print the return shipping label.
5. Ship the item within **7 days of initiating the return**.

---

# 6. Refund Policy

### 6.1 Refund Eligibility

Refunds are issued once the returned item has been:

* Received by our warehouse
* Inspected for damage or misuse

### 6.2 Refund Timeline

Refund processing times:

| Payment Method | Refund Time        |
| -------------- | ------------------ |
| Credit Card    | 5–10 business days |
| Debit Card     | 5–10 business days |
| PayPal         | 3–5 business days  |
| Store Credit   | Immediate          |

### 6.3 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if:

* Items are returned damaged
* Items are missing parts
* Items show signs of use

---

# 7. Exchanges

We currently allow exchanges for:

* Defective items
* Incorrect items shipped

Customers must request exchanges within **30 days of delivery**.

The replacement item will be shipped **after the original item is received**.

---

# 8. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If a customer receives:

* A damaged product
* The wrong product

They must contact support within **7 days of delivery**.

Customers may be asked to provide:

* Photos of the product
* Photos of packaging
* Order number

Replacement shipments are typically processed within **2 business days**.

---

# 9. Lost or Stolen Packages

If tracking shows delivered but the customer cannot locate the package:

1. Ask the customer to check with neighbors or building management.
2. Wait **48 hours** for possible carrier updates.
3. Contact support again if the package remains missing.

If the carrier confirms the package is lost, AssisMarket may:

* Issue a replacement shipment, or
* Provide a full refund.

---

# 10. Warranty Policy

Certain electronics include a **manufacturer warranty** ranging from **6–24 months**.

Warranty claims may require:

* Proof of purchase
* Product serial number
* Description of the issue

Support agents may direct customers to the manufacturer if required.

---

# 11. Customer Account Issues

### Password Reset

Customers may reset their password using the **Forgot Password** option on the login page.

### Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for:

* Fraudulent transactions
* Abuse of return policies
* Violations of platform terms

Customers may contact support to appeal a suspension.

# 13. Policy Exceptions

In special circumstances, supervisors may approve exceptions including:

* Extended return windows
* Refunds without returns
* Expedited replacement shipments

All exceptions must be documented in the support ticket system.
